Graphics Processing Unit (GPU)

GPUs specialise in processing graphics and outputting display. They are used in a variety of devices from computers to mobile phones! When you plug a monitor into a computer it is likely you will need to plug the cable into your graphics card, this could be a VGA or a HDMI cable. When graphical processing is taking place the GPU is using cuda cores to process the graphics.
