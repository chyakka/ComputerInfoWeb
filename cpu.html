Central Processing Unit (CPU)

Processors are an important part of the computer, they do all the calculations and are the brain of the computer. They are placed into a socket in the motherboard, having latches to seal them into place. Their speed is what determines how quickly they can calculate, this is measured in hertz. Not a lot of companies manufactor processors and the two most common manufacturers are Intel and AMD.
